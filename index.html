Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet in our solar system and the closest to the Sun. Its orbit around the Sun takes only 88 Earth days, the shortest of all the planets in the Solar System. Mercury is only slightly larger than Earth's Moon. From the surface of the planet, the Sun appears about four times as large as it does when viewed from Earth, and would appear as an enormous disk about 70 times the diameter of the full Moon.
